Quickstart

ClinGen Allele Registry provides unique and dereferenceable identifiers for alleles. The Allele Registry services are built on fast and high availability REST-APIs. Detailed description of these REST-APIs are provided in documentation hosted here.

We love to hear from our REST-API Users

If you plan to use REST-APIs and/or CAIds on regular basis in your system, do notify us. That will not only motivate us to develop outstanding services but scale up the infrastructure accordingly. We are just a mail away ! [bcm.clingen@gmail.com]
